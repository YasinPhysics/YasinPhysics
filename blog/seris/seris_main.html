    Episode 1: Introduction to Quantum Mechanics

    February 18, 2026 • 10 min read

    Quantum mechanics is the foundation of modern physics. It breaks the rules of classical physics and provides a new perspective on reality.

    What is Quantum Mechanics?

    Quantum mechanics is a theory that explains the behavior of very small particles (atoms, electrons, photons). It is fundamentally different from classical mechanics (Newton's laws).

    Mathematical Foundation

    $$ E = h\nu $$

    Planck's quantum hypothesis: Energy comes in discrete quanta.

    Wave-Particle Duality

    Light behaves as both particle and wave — this is the core idea of quantum mechanics.
