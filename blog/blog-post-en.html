The Mystery of Quantum Entanglement

February 18, 2026 • 8 min read

Quantum entanglement is one of the strangest concepts in quantum mechanics. Einstein called it "spooky action at a distance".

What is Entanglement?

When two particles are entangled, measuring one instantly determines the state of the other.

Mathematical Form

$$ |\psi\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \left( |0\rangle_A |1\rangle_B - |1\rangle_A |0\rangle_B \right) $$

Here A and B are two entangled particles.

Another Example

$$ E = mc^2 $$

Inline: The energy is $E = mc^2$.
